BMW-Z4 is an Angular based Web-Application that allows you to manage incidents.

Purpose

This application aims to provide a way to interact with users through user reports to determine when something goes wrong and fix it as soon as possible so that business can return to normal.​

  

Scope

Incident management includes any event which disrupts, or something which is capable of causing a disruption to the service.

  

Value

Ability to mitigate the risk of unplanned costs and labor for both business and IT support staff. Ability to detect and resolve incidents, which in turn results in lower downtime to the business, which means increased availability of the service.
